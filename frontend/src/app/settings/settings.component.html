How To Connect LeetCode

You need two cookie values from your logged-in LeetCode browser session: LEETCODE_SESSION and csrftoken.

Chrome / Edge Steps

  1. Log in to https://leetcode.com in your browser.
  2. Press F12 (or right-click and choose Inspect).
  3. Open Application tab.
  4. In left panel: Storage -> Cookies -> https://leetcode.com.
  5. Find row LEETCODE_SESSION and copy its Value.
  6. Find row csrftoken and copy its Value.
  7. Paste both values here and click Connect Once.

Firefox Steps

  1. Log in to https://leetcode.com.
  2. Open DevTools and go to Storage tab.
  3. Open Cookies -> https://leetcode.com.
  4. Copy LEETCODE_SESSION and csrftoken values.
  5. Paste and connect in this page.

Troubleshooting

  • If connect fails, re-login to LeetCode and recopy cookies.
  • Do not paste extra spaces before or after cookie values.
  • If submissions suddenly stop working later, your session likely expired; reconnect with fresh values.
Treat these cookie values like a password. Do not share them with anyone.
